As a professional dance choreographer, I have trained with variety of people with different ages and back grounds. Collaborating with other dancers to create performances that I have imagined, and watching my vision come to reality has always fascinated me.

I am currently working with a competetive team called 909 Hip Hip Troupe at University of California Riverside. My job is to create choreographies and train dancers with execution, body angles, and the purpose of movement. I also direct competition sets and prepare the team for an advanced performance with high quality. Additionally, I have been working with a company named Steezy Studio, where I record my choreographies and upload it to the company's website, in order for other people who don't have time to go to studios to learn the pieces anytime and anywhere.

My ultimate goal is to provide free dance classes for people who can't afford taking dance classes. With my passion in dance and my determination in helping others, I belive that I could bring a life-changing opportunities for people who share the same love and interests as me.
